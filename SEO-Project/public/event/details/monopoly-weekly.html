NBU Monopoly Sundays #123

  • Location: New Bulgarian University
  • Date: 01.01.2024 - 17:30
  • Entry: $34.99
Join us for an exciting Weekly Monopoly Tournament, where strategy, negotiation, and a little luck can make you the ultimate tycoon! Whether you're a seasoned player or a newcomer, this tournament offers fun and competition for all. Enjoy a friendly atmosphere with refreshments, meet fellow students that love boardgames almost as much as you do, and have a chance to win great prizes!

Here at NBU we have a long standing tradition of hosting Monopoly tournaments every Sunday. We have a dedicated room for the event, with plenty of tables and chairs for everyone. The event is open to all NBU students, and we encourage you to bring your friends along.

The prize pool for this event is $100, with the winner taking home $50, the runner-up $30, and the third place $20. The entry fee is $34.99, and you can pay it at the event. Food and drinks will be provided, so you can focus on the game and have a great time.

We hope to see you there! Let's roll the dice!
