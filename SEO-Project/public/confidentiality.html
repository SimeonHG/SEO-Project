Confidentiality Policy

We at our board games club and online store are committed to protecting the personal data of our customers and users. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data, as well as your rights regarding this information.

1. Collection of Personal Data

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide during registration, product orders, or when contacting us. This data may include your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and payment information.

2. Use of Personal Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:
  • Processing orders and deliveries
  • Providing information about products and services
  • Enhancing our services and improving your customer experience
  • Communicating with you about orders, promotions, and news
  • Administering your account and offering customer support

3. Sharing Personal Data

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:
  • With delivery services to fulfill your orders
  • With accounting and payment processors for payment handling
  • With government authorities as required by law

4. Security of Personal Data

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. Our systems are secured with state-of-the-art technologies.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of correction: You can request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right of deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Right to restriction: You can request the restriction of processing your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to portability: You can request your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

6. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to improve functionality and user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can set your browser to reject cookies or notify you when cookies are being sent.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Any changes will be published on this page and will take effect immediately upon posting. Please check this page regularly for updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us through the information provided on our contact page. Thank you for your trust and for being a part of our community!
